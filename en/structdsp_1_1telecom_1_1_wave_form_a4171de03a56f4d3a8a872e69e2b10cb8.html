Generation of I/Q samples from a binary stream

This function will create, from a binary sequence, I/Q samples. The following steps are done:

  • Constellation mapping,
  • Upsampling,
  • Shaping filter.
Parameters
bsBinary sequence.
ncoefsNumber of taps for shaping filter implementation.
osfOversampling factor.
[out]delayDelay, in number of output samples, between the beginning of the output I/Q stream, and the middle of the first symbol (the delay is due to the shaping filter).
