Computes the delay of a FIR upsampling filter

If the number of coefficients, \(K\) is a multiple or \(R\):

\[ \tau = \frac{K - 1}{2} \]

Otherwise:

\[ \tau = \frac{K - 1}{2} + R - (K [R]) \]
