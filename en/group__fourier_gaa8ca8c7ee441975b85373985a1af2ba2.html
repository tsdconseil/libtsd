auto dsp::fourier::ifft ( const Vector< T > &  X)

Inverse Fast discrete Fourier Transform

This function will compute the normalized DFT inverse of a real or complex vector:

\[ X_n = \frac{1}{\sqrt{N}}\cdot\sum_{k=0}^{N-1} x_k \cdot e^{\frac{+2\pi\mathbf{i}kn}{N}} \]

Example
Veccf x1 = Veccf::Random(100);
Veccf X = fft(x1);
Vecf x2 = ifft(X);
assert((x2-x1).norm2() < 1e-7);
