Poles / zeros diagram

The transfert function is factored as:

\[ H(z) = \frac{\prod z - z_i}{\prod z - p_i} \]

where the \(z_i\) and \(p_i\) are called respectively the zeros et poles.

htransfert function
figFigure where to plot the zeros and poles.
Example
// Moyenne glissante d'ordre 10
ArrayXf h = ArrayXf::Ones(10) / 10;
Figure f;
plot_plz(f, FRat<float>::rif(h));
