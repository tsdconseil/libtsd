Decimation of a column vector

From a vector \((x_k), k=0\dots n-1\), returns a subset of it, decimated by a factor of \(R\):

\[ y_k = x_{kR},\quad k =0 \dots \left\lfloor N/R\right\rfloor -1 \]

Example:
soit x = linspace(1, 20, 20);
soit y = sousech(x, 2);
Figure f;
f.plot(x, x, "b-", "x");
f.plot(y, y, "or", "y");
