Description détaillée

Modèle général, non linéaire :

\[ \begin{align}x_n &= f(x_{n-1}) + v_n\\ y_n &= g(x_n) + w_n\end{align} \]

Where \(u_n\) is the system input, \(x_n\) the system state, \(y_n\) the system output (observation), and \(v_n\) and \(w_n\) are white gaussian noise processes of covariance matrix \(Q\) and \(R\).
